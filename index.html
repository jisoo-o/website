Jisoo Park

I am a Ph.D student in Computer Vision and Machine Learning (CVML) Lab at Chung-Ang University, advised by Junseok Kwon. I completed M.S in Artificial Intelligence and B.S in Computer Science and Engineering at the same institution.

My research interests include audio-visual learning (speech enhancement, separation, active speaker detection) and 3D talking head generation.

My goal is to address diverse in the wild challenges and advance my research toward human-centered AI.
