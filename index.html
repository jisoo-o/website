Jisoo Park

I am a Ph.D student in Computer Vision and Machinie Learning(CVML) Lab at Chung-Ang University, advised by Prof. Junseok Kwon. I completed M.S in Artificial Intelligence and B.S in Computer Science and Engineering at the same institution.

My research interests include speech processing and 3D computer vision, with a particular interest in integrating audio and visual modalities. I am currently focusing on enhancing speech recognition and separation, as well as reconstructing 3D talking heads using Neural Radiance Fields.

My goal is to address diverse in the wild challenges and advance my research toward practical AI solutions.
